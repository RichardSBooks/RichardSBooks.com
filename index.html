About the Author

Hi, I'm Richard.

I design notebooks, activity books, and lighthearted titles that are meant to be used—not just collected. Whether it’s for writing, relaxing, or having a quick laugh, each book is created with a focus on clarity, usability, and clean design.

My goal is simple: to offer practical, well-designed books that fit easily into everyday life without overcomplicating things.

All titles are independently published through Amazon KDP, allowing for print-on-demand production and reliable delivery across the US and internationally.
